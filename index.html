Privacy Policy for Nebula

Effective Date: January 22, 2026 Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Nebula ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Nebula Chrome Extension.

1. Information Collection and Use

Nebula is a visual customization tool for Dream Journey AI.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or location.
  • No External Transmission: All data related to your custom skins, colors, and settings stay on your local device. We do not use external servers to store your preferences.

✨ Your Privacy is Protected: Everything you create in Nebula stays on your device. We never see your customizations or settings.

2. Permissions and Data Handling

To provide its core functionality, Nebula requires specific permissions:

  • Storage: We use chrome.storage.local (and optionally chrome.storage.sync) to save your custom themes, font choices, and skin configurations locally on your device.
  • Host Permissions (dreamjourneyai.com): This is used solely to inject custom CSS and modify the visual appearance of the Dream Journey AI interface as per your settings. We do not read your private chat logs or account information.
  • Active Tab: Used only to apply your visual customizations to the active Dream Journey AI tab when you interact with the extension.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Nebula does not include any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts.

Google Fonts

When you select Google Fonts for customization, font files are loaded directly from Google's CDN. This follows Google Fonts' privacy policy and is a standard practice for web font delivery.

4. Data Security

All customization data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API. You have complete control over your data:

  • You can export your skins at any time
  • You can delete skins individually
  • All data is removed when you uninstall the extension
  • Chrome sync settings respect your browser's sync preferences

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or concerns about how Nebula handles data, you may contact us via the support email provided on the Chrome Web Store listing.

🔒 Your Trust Matters: We're committed to transparency. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out.
