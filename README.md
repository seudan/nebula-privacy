# Privacy Policy for Nebula
Effective Date: January 22, 2026 / Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Nebula ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Nebula Chrome Extension.

1. Information Collection and Use
Nebula is a visual customization tool for Dream Journey AI.
- No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or location.
- No External Transmission: All data related to your custom skins, colors, and settings stay on your local device. We do not use external servers to store your preferences.

2. Permissions and Data Handling
To provide its core functionality, Nebula requires specific permissions:
- Storage: We use `chrome.storage.local` (and optionally `chrome.storage.sync`) to save your custom themes, font choices, and skin configurations.
- Host Permissions (dreamjourneyai.com): This is used solely to inject custom CSS and modify the visual appearance of the Dream Journey AI interface as per your settings. We do not read your private chat logs or account information.

3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Nebula does not include any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts.

4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

5. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via the support email provided on the Chrome Web Store listing.
